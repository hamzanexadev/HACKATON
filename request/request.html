Assistant Guidance

Be Specific

Include clear details about your problem and what you've already tried. This helps helpers understand exactly what you need.

Set Clear Goals

Define what success looks like. Are you learning, building, or debugging? Do you need a quick fix or long-term mentoring?

Add Context

Share relevant background: your experience level, project scope, deadline, and any constraints. This helps match you with the right helper.

Be Realistic

Ask for help that one person can reasonably provide in a session or two. Massive projects work better with ongoing partnerships.
